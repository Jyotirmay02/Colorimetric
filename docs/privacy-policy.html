Privacy Policy

Chromalyze

Effective date: 1 June 2026

Chromalyze (“the App”) is a chemistry-research utility developed by Blue Lotus Lab as an academic project at Maharaja Purna Chandra Autonomous College, Baripada, Odisha, India. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App handles and how that data is stored.

1. Data we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personally identifiable information.

  • We do not require account creation or sign-in.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, advertising identifiers, or analytics events.
  • We do not use third-party analytics, tracking SDKs, or advertising networks.

2. Camera and Photo Library permissions

The App requests two device permissions, used strictly for the App's core scientific function:

  • Camera — to capture an image of a chemical solution sample that the user wishes to analyse.
  • Photo Library / Storage — to allow the user to import an existing solution image from their device gallery.

Captured or imported images are processed entirely on the user's device. They are not uploaded to any server controlled by us.

3. Where your data is stored

  • All calibration samples, prediction history, settings, and image data are stored locally on the device using the operating system's secure local storage (AsyncStorage).
  • The data never leaves the device unless the user themselves explicitly exports it (e.g. by tapping the CSV export button and choosing to share or save the file).
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally-stored data permanently.

4. Network usage

The App contacts a backend service operated by the developers for the sole purpose of extracting RGB pixel values from an image region selected by the user. The image's base64 representation is sent for processing and the extracted numeric RGB values are returned. No image, RGB result, or request metadata is logged, stored, or shared by us beyond the duration of the single API call.

5. Children's privacy

The App is intended for use by researchers, students, and educators. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date.

7. Contact
